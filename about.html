History of MEME



The school was originally a club held by 3 men to talk about elaborate and detailed plans about grandiose architecture. Its first meeting was held
on March 10, 1990 in a small room in the building of Dickinson where the friends of those 3 friends also joined in their meetings.

As the club expanded, several people became curious about what the world of engineering had to offer for plans of architectural builds and plans
were almost always talked about. Because of this, the three men and their friends became teachers to those who had little to no knowledge of math
and engineering and replaced the club name with "Dysoni's Circle" and rebranded the club into a sort of school on February 11, 1992.

Over time, the three men eventually quit these jobs, eventually handing them down to several people, one of them being Mr. Bendover Wallace, who
had a passion for all things mathematics. He started to transform the empty Dickinson building into a school. Its first building was completed on
January 21, 1995 known as "Origin" where it was officially recognized as a school. Over the course of the next 5 years, 4 more buildings were
established and it was finally called "Manila Education in Mathematics and Engineering".

Currently, several new branches and courses for learning have been implemented, sort of contradicting its name of it only being Mathematics and
Engineering. Debates have appeared over a new nickname that the present school Director, Dr. Bang Chow, has suggested. It being "Memion" as homage
to its abbreviation of "MEME" combined with the suffix "-ion" to suggest a whole other life as a university. But its name will stay, for now.
