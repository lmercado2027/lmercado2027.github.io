This is My Battle Anthem

Author: Crisyhan D. Edejer

Like a boat in the middle of the sea,
Making waves and sending them in motion,
But with enough courage and devotion,
I'll get back to shore just you wait and see,

I just want to get it all out tonight,
Because all those things I could never say,
Are rotting my mind like an endless plague,
I really hope you can hear me this time;

At the end I see the light,
I know that it won't take long,
Because I can be alright,

If I get ahold of life
I know that I can stay strong,
For this is my song of fight.

I Shall Survive

Author: Crisyhan D. Edejer

When you had left, you had made me terrified,
Did not think I would last long without your touch,
But how you've done me wrong, I've thought about all night,
And so I realized that I never needed you that much.

So now I see you crawling back to me,
But I still think about your sins now and then.
If I'd known you'd return I would have thrown the key,
Just so that I wouldn't have to face you again.

Turn around now, and walk through the door,
Just like you did when you told me goodbye.
Trust me, you're not welcome here anymore,
When you left me here did you think I would lay and die?

No, for as long as I love I'll stay alive,
For I have a life to live, and love to give, I shall survive.

Groove to the Beat

Author: Lorenzo S. Mercado

Although I have been struggling from birth
I'm simply having fun throughout this life
Because dancing is the way I survive
No matter who you are or what you're worth

I know we're staying alive on this earth
Life may be good or bad, but we still thrive
As we are certainly staying alive
So just dance away and be full of mirth

But then the eternity dawns on me
As I see no path for my own future
I need to ask for some help to see

What it requires for me to be free
For now, I will keep on dancing for sure
And I will groove to the beat, just to be

For Freedom, We Fight

Author: Lorenzo S. Mercado

In the dark and gloomy sight of warfare
A brave warrior seeks to restore freedom
When he summons people, we will go there
Keep yourself together, for this kingdom

Even in the darkest times, we won't give up
Let us all unite and fight as we should
For all evil we see, we will disrupt
Fighting for freedom and for common good

So we finally see the light of day
As our fellow soldiers rise from darkness
Gather around here; come hear, feel and stay
We have restored our life in its fullness

At last, this brave warrior has helped us win
Fulfilling our desire that burned within

Still On My Feet, I Am

Author: Matteo Andres R. Redillas

Thou shall not ever know what it is like
Thine blood, just like winter, a frozen cold
From behind you, a cold, lonesome light glowed
Behind a facade, in which thou art hiding

A dunce deprived of victory, thought thee
Go and observe, for once more, here I am
I hold love, a flavor, through a simple path
Whilst on my feet, perhaps how, thou may seek

Nothingness, thou has vanished to
Still on my feet, I am; hast thou not been informed?
A survivor I feel, seeming like a kid

Up on my feet, all of time I went through
Thought of thee, gone, my mind to reform
Yes, yes, yes, I am upright still

Amidst the Infernos and Blaze

Author: Matteo Andres R. Redillas

Waves of demonic laughs surround the night
Battling the foe, this hellscape we go around
Corpses on the beach, driven souls ignite
On hell's darkest fields, passing shapes we scout

In blazes and sorrows, we know once more
With all our granted freedom, we now fly
Free in front of this thundering pour
The trek goes on, for we go forth to the wilds

Distant from the moon's beam and the sun's set
Within every heart and every spirit
Away, we bide the day that is not yet
Lives who've departed, in vain and missing

We feel life's pain, gone in ten hundred days
We go on, amidst infernos and blaze
